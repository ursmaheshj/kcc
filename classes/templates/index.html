About
KCC

We are pioneer and one of the determined coaching institute in kandhar.
With an impressive track record molding over 50 NMMS scholarship holders.

Kagne coaching institute was founded in year 2014. We are famous for 1st to 10th standard classes in Marathi, Semi-English and CBSE. Alongside we also provide coaching for Navodaya, Scholarship and Foundation batches.

Apart from full time coaching, we also offer distance learning mode hence not just the students of kandhar but students from all over Maharashtra can study from KCC.
